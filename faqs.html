Digital Copies & Guestbook

Do you provide digital copies of the photos?

+

Yes, digital copies can be sent to the event hosts after the event.

Do you offer a guestbook?

+

Yes! We offer a post-event guestbook add-on. After your event, we use all the digital photos captured in the booth to create a beautifully designed, professionally printed photo album. Once the guestbook is completed, it's shipped directly to you as a lasting keepsake.

Booking & Payment

Do you require a deposit?

+

Yes, a 50% non-refundable retainer fee is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is due one week before your event.

Is the deposit refundable?

+

No. The 50% deposit is non-refundable and is required to secure your event date.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking?

+

If you cancel your event, the 50% deposit is forfeited. Any additional payments made beyond the deposit will be refunded.

Can I reschedule my event?

+

Yes. You may reschedule once at no extra charge as long as the new date is available. Your deposit will transfer to the new date.

What if the new date I want isn't available?

+

If your requested date is unavailable, the booking will be considered a cancellation, and the deposit will be forfeited.
