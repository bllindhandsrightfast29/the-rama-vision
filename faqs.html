Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photography services

General Questions

How do I book a session?

+

Simply visit our Contact page and fill out the inquiry form, or email us directly at Theramavision@gmail.com. We'll respond within 24 hours to discuss your needs and check our availability.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve the local area but are available for travel anywhere. Travel fees may apply for destinations outside our standard service area. Contact us to discuss your location.

Do you require a deposit?

+

Yes, we require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before your event. We accept all major credit cards, checks, and electronic payments.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Deposits are non-refundable. If you need to reschedule, we're happy to work with you to find a new date within one year of your original booking, subject to availability.

Wedding Photography

How long does it take to receive our wedding photos?

+

You'll receive a sneak peek of 10-15 images within 48 hours. The full gallery of professionally edited images will be delivered within 4-6 weeks of your wedding date.

Can we customize a wedding package?

+

Absolutely! We understand every wedding is unique. We can create a custom package tailored to your specific needs, timeline, and budget. Contact us to discuss your vision.

Do you photograph destination weddings?

+

Yes! We love destination weddings. Travel expenses (airfare, accommodation, and meals) are additional to our photography packages. We'll provide a detailed quote based on your location.

Will you scout our venue before the wedding?

+

For premium and elite packages, we offer a complimentary venue visit to scout locations and plan the perfect shots. This ensures we're fully prepared on your big day.

Corporate & Events

What types of corporate events do you cover?

+

We cover conferences, seminars, trade shows, corporate parties, team-building events, product launches, and business meetings. We also offer professional headshot sessions for teams.

Can you provide same-day images for social media?

+

Yes! Our Corporate Pro and Conference packages include same-day preview images perfect for immediate social media posting. We can deliver edited highlights within hours of your event.

Do you offer commercial licensing?

+

Yes, all corporate packages include a commercial use license, allowing you to use the images for marketing, advertising, websites, and publications.

Sports Photography

What sports do you photograph?

+

We photograph all sports including football, basketball, soccer, baseball, track and field, swimming, volleyball, and more. Our high-speed equipment captures every crucial moment.

Can parents purchase individual photos?

+

Yes! We set up a private online gallery where parents and supporters can view and purchase prints, digital downloads, and photo packages of their athletes.

Do you shoot indoor and outdoor sports?

+

Absolutely! We have professional equipment for both indoor and outdoor venues, including specialized low-light gear for indoor gymnasiums and arenas.

Drone Photography

Are you FAA certified for drone operations?

+

Yes, our drone pilots are FAA Part 107 certified and fully insured. We operate legally and safely in compliance with all federal and local regulations.

What can you photograph with a drone?

+

Real estate, landscapes, events, weddings, construction sites, agricultural land, commercial properties, and more. Drone photography provides stunning perspectives impossible to achieve from the ground.

What if weather conditions aren't suitable for flying?

+

Safety is our priority. If weather conditions (high winds, rain, etc.) make flying unsafe, we'll reschedule for a suitable date at no additional charge.

Deliverables & Usage

How are photos delivered?

+

All photos are delivered through a private online gallery where you can view, download, share, and order prints. Select packages also include a USB drive with all high-resolution files.

Can we share and print the photos?

+

Yes! All packages include a print release, giving you full rights to print and share your images for personal use (commercial use requires appropriate licensing).

Do you edit all the photos?

+

Yes, all delivered images are professionally edited including color correction, exposure adjustment, and artistic enhancements. We provide only our best work.

How long do you keep our photos?

+

We maintain backups of all photos for one year after delivery. We recommend downloading and backing up your images once you receive them to ensure you have permanent access.
